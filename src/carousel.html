Seasonal Haikus

Spring

Cherry blossoms bloom,
Soft whispers of a new life,
Springtime's sweet embrace.

Summer

Golden sun ablaze,
Children's laughter fills the air,
Endless summer days.

Autumn

Leaves of red and gold,
Cool breeze whispers, seasons change,
Autumn's song unfolds.

Winter

Snowflakes gently fall,
Silent world, a soft white shroud,
Winter's calm embrace.
